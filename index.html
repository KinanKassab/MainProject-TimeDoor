About Kinan Kassab:

Code, Curiosity, and a Decade of Tinkering

I wrote my first lines of code at age ten and never really stopped. The journey began with C++ and C# console projects, evolved into UI experiments, and about eighteen months ago accelerated into full-on web development. I still chase that feeling of solving a puzzle at 2 a.m.—only now the puzzles look a lot better on screen.

Early Sparks

Childhood me thought `cout` or `cin` were magic. I spent weekends wiring up tiny C++ and C# apps, learning that loops, logic, and stubbornness could build anything.

Leveling Up

That foundation made the jump to web development feel natural. When I dove in a year and a half ago, the pace felt intense—in a good way. HTML, CSS, and JavaScript quickly became new favorite toys.

Why I Stay

I genuinely enjoy translating ideas into experiences. Clean UI, ethical tech, friendly microinteractions, those details matter. And yes, I still celebrate every bug fix with suspiciously fancy coffee.
